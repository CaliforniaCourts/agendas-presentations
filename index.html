What was your favorite part of the meetup?

I really enjoyed being able to meet developers from other counties, along with having the opportunity to see what other devs were working on! The swag was pretty awesome too...

What was your least desirable part of the meetup?

None, but round table could be longer and more organized. We didn't get to talk to everyone.
I felt it was mostly demos

What topics would you like to see the future?

I'd be interested in a project relevant tools approach: such as how are the courts coding their other projects, websites,...: aspx, bootstrap, jquery, ... is there a possibility of developing a common .css for that? Could CourtDev have a github shared resource site, for common projects
A deeper dive into fewer topics. Presentations were great for broad exposure but felt lacking in details. Possibly some discussion on architecture and best practices.
